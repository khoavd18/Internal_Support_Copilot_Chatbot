# Customer Risk Policy

## Risk Signals

Customer risk can come from SLA breaches, unresolved incidents, repeated integration failures, security blockers, billing disputes, sentiment drops, or executive escalation.

## Risk Scores

Scores above 85 are critical and require leadership awareness. Scores from 70 to 84 are high and require active mitigation. Scores from 40 to 69 require monitoring.

## Evidence

Risk explanations should cite the ticket, risk event, policy, service, and engineering issue that support the assessment.

## Mitigation

Recommended actions should be specific and owned by a team. Do not claim risk is mitigated unless the source event status supports it.
