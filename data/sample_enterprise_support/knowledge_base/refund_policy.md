# Refund And Credit Policy

## Refund Eligibility

Pilot invoices may be refunded when the pilot was canceled before production activation. Duplicate seat charges may be refunded after billing verifies the renewal ledger.

## SLA Credits

SLA credits require an approved incident or breach timeline. Support should review the SLA policy and incident evidence before confirming credit eligibility.

## Tax Exemption

Customers should upload tax exemption certificates through billing settings. Support can confirm receipt but should not approve tax status without billing review.

## Customer Response

Customer-facing replies should explain what is eligible, what evidence is needed, and which team owns final approval.
