# Access And Permission Policy

## Admin Requests

Support may assist account admins with role checks, permission refreshes, and invite issues. Support must verify the requester identity before changing any access state.

## Billing Owner Access

Billing owner access can be restored after confirming the requester belongs to the account and has authorization from an existing admin or account owner.

## Integration Access

OAuth callback and token issues should be routed to integration support when a customer application cannot authorize or sync.

## Internal Notes

Internal troubleshooting notes must not be included in customer-facing replies unless rewritten into customer-safe language.
