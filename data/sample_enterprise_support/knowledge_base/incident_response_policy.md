# Incident Response Policy

## Declaration

Declare an incident when a production customer workflow is unavailable, data correctness is at risk, or repeated failures affect a strategic account.

## Ownership

The incident commander owns coordination. The service owner owns technical mitigation. Support owns customer communication and evidence collection.

## Updates

For `sev1` incidents, update affected customers at a regular cadence. Updates should include known impact, current mitigation, and next update time.

## Review

After mitigation, prepare a customer-safe incident summary with timeline, cause, remediation, and preventive actions.
