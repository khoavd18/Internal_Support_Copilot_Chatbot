# Enterprise Support Policy

## Coverage

Enterprise customers receive priority routing, incident escalation support, and coordinated account owner communication for high impact issues.

## Escalation Criteria

Escalate when an enterprise account has a `p1`, a breached `p2`, repeated integration failure, security concern, or risk score above 75.

## Account Owner Coordination

Support should notify the account owner when an issue affects production operations, renewal confidence, or executive sponsor trust.

## Response Style

Responses should be concise, evidence-based, and customer-safe. Avoid internal labels unless they are already customer visible.
