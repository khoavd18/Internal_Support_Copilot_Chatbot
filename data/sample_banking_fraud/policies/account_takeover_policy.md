# Synthetic Account Takeover Policy

## Purpose

This synthetic policy describes account takeover handling for demo banking data.

## ATO Signals

Unknown devices, foreign IP sessions, impossible travel, password reset activity, and sudden high-value transfers can indicate account takeover.

## Analyst Actions

Require step-up authentication and contact the customer through a verified channel. For high or critical cases, freeze risky payment rails such as online wires until the customer confirms activity.
