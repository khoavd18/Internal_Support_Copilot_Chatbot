# Synthetic Card Fraud Policy

## Purpose

This synthetic policy describes card fraud handling for portfolio demos only.

## Card Testing And Ecommerce Risk

Multiple small digital-goods purchases, rapid declines, and high-value ecommerce transactions from a new device can indicate card testing or account misuse.

## Analyst Actions

Apply temporary ecommerce controls when card testing signals are active. Contact the customer through a verified channel before restoring full card capability. Do not reveal detection thresholds to the customer.
